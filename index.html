About

Alexlune is an webapp developed by Renato Araujo. So called in tribute for the great Professor Alexandre Zabot, UFSC.
The pourpose is to imitate an old simulator, made using Flash, an old Web Technology, no more available for public. I hope it helps to spreed scientific education worldwide, as well as support more enjoyable and clear learning on this theme.
